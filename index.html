Introduction

In this project we will analyse data from a quantified self project, in which 6 participants try to quantify how well do they perform an activity rather than how much of the activity they do. Participants were asked to perform barbell lifts correctly and incorrectly in 5 different ways, and data from accelerometers on the belt, forearm, arm, and dumbell were recorded. See more info on the Weight Lifting Exercise Dataset.

We will use the training dataset from here and the testing dataset from here.

We will predict the manner in which they did the exercise, through analysing the classe variable in the training set, and use a prediction model to predict 20 different test cases.
